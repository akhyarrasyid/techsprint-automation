# Inventory Rules — Kopikita Roastery Inventory Planning

## Rumus Safety Stock Kopikita
Safety Stock = 1.65 × std_demand_harian × sqrt(lead_time_hari)
Faktor 1.65 = 95% service level (z-score distribusi normal).

## Rumus Reorder Point (ROP)
ROP = (avg_demand_harian × lead_time_hari) + safety_stock
Ketika current_stock ≤ ROP → harus buat Purchase Order segera.

## Klasifikasi Status Inventaris Kopikita
- HEALTHY: current_stock > ROP — stok aman, tidak perlu tindakan
- WARNING: safety_stock ≤ current_stock ≤ ROP — disarankan PO segera
- CRITICAL: current_stock < safety_stock — risiko stockout tinggi, PO DARURAT
